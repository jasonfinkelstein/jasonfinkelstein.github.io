Hi! I'm Jason, a full-stack JavaScript developer.

I'm a full-stack web developer with an unconventional path into tech. I originally studied biology with plans to go to medical school, and after graduating, I worked in a medical reference lab analyzing samples and tracking infection trends in New York. But while prepping for the MCAT, I had a realization—I wasn’t pursuing medicine for the right reasons. That’s when I decided to follow a long-time interest in software development, and I haven’t looked back since. Now, I build web applications that blend logic, creativity, and problem-solving, and I love every part of it.
